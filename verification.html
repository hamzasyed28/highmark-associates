Buying property without proper verification can lead to serious financial and legal risks. Our comprehensive verification process protects your investment before any transaction proceeds.

Why Verification Matters

Protect Your Property Investment

Pakistan's property market can be complex, with risks including fraudulent documentation, disputed ownership, undisclosed liabilities, and properties sold without proper CDA approvals.

Our verification team reviews every document thoroughly before any transaction proceeds, giving you complete confidence in your investment decision.

🛡️

Risk Prevention

Identify and avoid fraudulent or disputed properties before investing your hard-earned money.

✅

Legal Clarity

Ensure the property has clear title, approved documentation, and no outstanding disputes or liabilities.

💼

Investor Protection

Especially critical for overseas Pakistanis who cannot personally inspect documentation and need trusted local verification.

Our Process

What We Verify

Our comprehensive checklist covers every aspect of property documentation and legal standing.

📄 Ownership Records & Title Deed
🏛️ CDA Approvals & Allotment Letters
📋 NOCs & Government Permissions

Step by Step

Our Verification Process

01

Document Collection

We collect all available property documents from the seller including title deed, allotment letter, and transfer history.

02

CDA & Registry Check

We verify the property's status directly with CDA and the relevant registry office to confirm approvals and ownership.

03

Legal & Tax Review

All outstanding taxes, dues, court orders, and encumbrances are identified and reviewed before proceeding.

04

Verification Report

We provide a clear summary of findings and advise you on whether to proceed, renegotiate, or avoid the transaction.
