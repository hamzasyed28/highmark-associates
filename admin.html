Why don't changes show on phone / other devices?

Changes you make in this admin panel are saved only to this browser's local storage. They are not automatically synced to your phone or any other device. To make your changes go live everywhere, follow these 3 steps:

  1. Step 1 — Download files: Click all the download buttons below to get the updated .js files.
  2. Step 2 — Replace files in your project: Copy the downloaded files into the js/ folder of your project on your computer, replacing the old ones.
  3. Step 3 — Push to GitHub: Commit and push to GitHub. GitHub Pages will update within 1–2 minutes and changes will appear everywhere — desktop, mobile, everywhere.
