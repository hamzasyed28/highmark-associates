Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Highmark Associates cover? +

We specialize in E-11, D-12, F-10, F-11 in Islamabad, as well as DHA Phase 2 Islamabad and Bahria Town Rawalpindi. We also handle properties in selected other sectors on request.

Can I buy property remotely as an overseas Pakistani? +

Yes! We have a dedicated overseas service where we handle the entire process via Power of Attorney (POA). We provide video tours, legal verification, and full documentation support so you can invest safely from abroad.

Do you verify property documents before listing? +

Absolutely. Every property on our platform goes through a verification process that includes title deed checks, CDA approval status, encumbrance verification, and site visits by our team. We do not list properties with uncertain documentation.

What are your agency fees? +

Our fees are transparent and discussed upfront before any commitment. We typically charge a standard brokerage commission only upon successful transaction completion. Contact us directly for details specific to your requirements.

How quickly can I get a property valuation? +

We can usually provide an initial market valuation within 24–48 hours for most sectors we cover. For a detailed report, our team will arrange a site visit and provide a comprehensive assessment within 3–5 working days.
